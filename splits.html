What is a split?

A split sheet is a flash painting that is split between two different artists using a certain theme. Usually, one artist will paint on half of a large sheet of paper, sign it, and then ship it to another artist. Once the other artist receives that painting, they will paint the same design again on the other traded sheet. After both of the paintings are finished, it will create a beautiful flash sheet between two artists.


On this page are the available artists that are looking to create split sheets. In order to reach out to other artists, you must have an account with flashpaitners.art. Please sign in or register to start creating with other artists!
